Who We Are

TFG General Trading is a leading global trading company offering a wide range of services and products across industries. We connect markets through quality, efficiency, and trust, maintaining the highest standards of business ethics and customer satisfaction.

Our Vision

To become a globally recognized leader in trading and supply chain solutions, driven by innovation, integrity, and customer excellence.

Our Mission

To empower businesses and industries by delivering reliable trading solutions, superior products, and exceptional value built on transparency and trust.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity: We conduct every transaction with honesty and fairness.
  • Innovation: We seek new and better ways to serve our clients.
  • Commitment: We dedicate ourselves to our partners’ success.
  • Teamwork: We collaborate to achieve excellence.

Our Teams

Our dedicated departments drive the success of TFG General Trading through professionalism and collaboration.

  • Finance: Managing accounts, budgets, and company profitability.
  • Import & Export: Handling international trading, logistics, and supply chain management.
  • IT & Procurement: Implementing technology solutions and managing vendor sourcing.
  • Cleaning: Maintaining hygiene and cleanliness in all facilities.
  • Human Resource: Fostering talent, growth, and employee welfare.
  • Store Keeper: Managing inventory, warehousing, and product flow.
  • General Service: Providing support services that keep operations efficient.

Growth Timeline

2020: TFG General Trading was founded with a small but passionate team.
2021: Expanded into international trading and import/export services.
2023: Formed partnerships with top manufacturers and distributors.
2025: Recognized among the top emerging trading companies in the region.
